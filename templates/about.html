About Us

Welcome to our American Sign Language Detection Website, Signify!

Together, we combine expertise, creativity, and a commitment to meaningful impact, striving to build solutions that empower and connect communities worldwide.Signify would not be where it is today without the invaluable guidance and unwavering support of our mentor, Prof. Deepali Shrikhande. Her expertise, encouragement, and belief in our vision have been instrumental in shaping our journey.

At Signify, we are a close-knit team of two passionate individuals, driven by a shared dedication to data science, machine learning, and innovation. Our mission is to break barriers and bridge gaps through technology, making information and communication accessible to everyone, including those who use sign languages.
